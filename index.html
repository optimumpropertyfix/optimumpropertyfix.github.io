The revolution is here.

Optimum Property Fix (OPF) aims to revamp the University of Nevada, Reno current system through a new and improved maintenance request system, accessible to dormitory students, facilities and service members, and administrators.

Audience

Students

OPF is available all dormitory students to create maintenance requests, submit feedback, and immediate assistance through OPF's application. 

Facilities and Services

OPF can facilitate communication through the facilities and services devices including scheduling of repairs and management of maintenance requests.

Administrators

OPF is available to administrators to view analytics, building health reports, and budget for costly upgrades. 

Features

Mobile

OPF can manage, measure, and optimize maintenance requests on any device. 

Chatbot

OPF includes a chatbot to help answer immediate questions. 

Customizable

OPF includes a customizable dashboard to rearrange items. 

Environmental

OPF reduces UNR's environmental footprint reducing paper requests. 

Centralized System

OPF brings together housing forms providing a more manageable system. 

Feedback

OPF gives immediate assessment on completed work and maintenance request.
